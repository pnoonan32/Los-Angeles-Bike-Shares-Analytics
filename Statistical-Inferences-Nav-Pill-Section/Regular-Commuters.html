Inferences

It is clearly evident that Monthly Passes are far more popular than Flex passes. But by a substantial amount. Over 65%, but how come. It may be from an economic or financial perspective. More specifically this answer comes down to how far, how much, and how often the consumer is willing to pay for the usage of bike shares. Flex Passes are for a yearly pass and monthly pass is monthly. It could have to do with the fact that people may feel as if the cost of a yearly pass is not worth and more flexibility is available with monthly passes.
