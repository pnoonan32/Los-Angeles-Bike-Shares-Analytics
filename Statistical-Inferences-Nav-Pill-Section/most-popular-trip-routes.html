Inferences

One way Trip Routes are by far the majority in this dataset. It's peak is Mid-August, and it's low is late-December to late-January. On average One way trip routes are about 75% more than Round Trip trip routes. This could be due to alternatives. Possibly after work consumers may feel an alternative method of transportation is more convenient for their purposes, or maybe the consumer only needs it to commute one way. It is evident that bike shares appear to be used more as convenience rather than dependent. More specifically, if consumers did not have access to bike shares they would have no issue finding an alternative.
